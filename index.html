About Ghanaman Association of USA

Have something to say? Contact Us anytime and share your thoughts.

  • Akwaaba Ghanaman

    Ghanaman Association of USA is a converged forum for Ghanaians community within New Jersey and its neighboring states. Ghanaman serves as an umbrella organization for Ghanaians living in New Jersey and its neighboring states with sole aim of acting as a mouthpiece for its members and Ghanaians community in New Jersey to enhance the living standards of Ghanaian-USA in New Jersey through social, cultural, and educational programs, and through other diaspora initiatives..

  • Humble Beginnings

    Ghanaman Association of USA takes pride in preserving Ghana culture whiles maintaining our true ancient cultural values and beliefs that have being passed down by our ancestors which our board members and its active members use to shape their lives and are openly ready to teach and mentor the Ghanaians and its younger generation living in New Jersey. The Association operates with ethics, honesty and decency in everything we do so we can uphold integrity and gain respect from members, soon to join and non-members..

  • Acknowledgement

    Ghana being 5169 miles away does not feel the same especially the thought of living behind loved ones, family and home. As a president, I believe that joining the Ghanaman Association of USA will give you a sense of belonging and part of an organization that truly cares for active members, soon to join members and the community in which they are situated.

  • Aims and Objectives

    The purpose of the Organization shall be to foster and promote the interest and understanding of its members.

    To enhance and encourage unity among its members.

    To educate and enhance the knowledge of its members

    To benefit members who are in need of legitimate assistance

    To perform social activities which may be of benefit to its members.
